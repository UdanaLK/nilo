Kottu Roti

Ingredients: Roti, vegetables, eggs, chicken.

Instructions: Cut the roti into small pieces. Stir-fry with vegetables, eggs, and chicken. Mix well and serve hot.

Fish Ambul Thiyal

Ingredients: Fish, goraka, garlic, pepper.

Instructions: Marinate fish with spices and goraka. Simmer on low heat until the fish is well-cooked.

Hoppers (Appa)

Ingredients: Rice flour, coconut milk, yeast.

Instructions: Mix the ingredients and ferment. Cook in a small wok until edges are crispy and the center is soft.

Pol Sambola

Ingredients: Grated coconut, red chili, lime juice.

Instructions: Mix grated coconut with chili and lime juice. Serve as a side dish.

Parippu (Dhal Curry)

Ingredients: Red lentils, coconut milk, spices.

Instructions: Boil lentils until soft. Add coconut milk and spices. Cook until thickened.

Kiribath (Milk Rice)

Ingredients: Rice, coconut milk, salt.

Instructions: Cook rice with coconut milk and salt. Shape into a cake and cut into pieces.

Lunu Miris

Ingredients: Red onion, chili, lime juice.

Instructions: Grind onions with chili and lime juice. Serve as a spicy condiment.

Watalappan

Ingredients: Coconut milk, jaggery, eggs.

Instructions: Mix coconut milk with jaggery and eggs. Steam until set.

Chicken Curry

Ingredients: Chicken, curry powder, coconut milk.

Instructions: Cook chicken with spices and coconut milk until tender.

Pittu

Ingredients: Rice flour, grated coconut, salt.

Instructions: Mix rice flour with coconut and steam. Serve with curry.

Beef Smore

Ingredients: Beef, onions, vinegar, spices.

Instructions: Marinate beef with vinegar and spices. Slow-cook with onions until tender and flavorful.

Seeni Sambol

Ingredients: Onions, chili powder, sugar, spices.

Instructions: Caramelize onions with chili powder and spices. Add sugar to balance the flavors. Serve as a sweet and spicy condiment.
